Why this works:
- w-full on the scroll container allows the panel to expand to fill available space when the sidebar collapses/expands.
- min-w-[700px] on the header, table, and footer ensures a usable minimum table width. Below that, overflow-x-auto provides horizontal scrolling.
- Header and table share the same min-width, so they scroll together with a single scrollbar and the header always spans the full table width.
