- min-w-0 overflow-hidden on the right section ensures the grid cell does not overflow its allocated space, while the inner overflow-x-auto handles horizontal scrolling.
- Removing w-full from the inner scroll container avoids conflicting width constraints; the grid cell width governs available space.
